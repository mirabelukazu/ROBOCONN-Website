What is RoboCONN?

Robotics Conference Of New eNgineers

Prepare yourselves for the Robotics event of the year. In collaboration with Brown Robotics Club, CURC will be hosting an intercollegiate robotics club showcase. The Robotics Conference of New eNgineers (ROBOCONN) exists to bring together college robotics groups to share their projects, learn from workshops, and make connections.
