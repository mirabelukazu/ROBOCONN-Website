Panel 3

Real-World Robotics

Earl Hall Schiff Room 5:00 – 5:45 pm

Emma Forman

DoorDash and Myca Collective

Emma Forman is the founder of Myca Collective, a global community for leading women in food and agriculture. She also serves on the product team at DoorDash Labs, where she’s helping build the first autonomous vehicle purpose-built for delivery. With over 7 years of experience across the food system, spanning nonprofits, CPG, retail, and logistics, Emma brings a cross-sector perspective on how technology can reshape the way food is grown, made, and moved.

Art Jiang (Fengyi Jiang)

Intuitive Surgical

Art Jiang (Fengyi Jiang) is a Machine Learning Engineer at Intuitive Surgical, working on real-time perception and GPU compute for the da Vinci 5 surgical robot. His research focuses on augmenting real-time surgical vision and decision-making through multi-modal sensing and computer vision. His work has been published at MICCAI and ECCV, including the SurgiSR4K dataset and SurgVerse, a novel view synthesis framework for endoscopic footage. He is an organizer of the ECCV 2026 workshop on Data Curation and Augmentation in Medical Imaging (DCA-MI), and holds an MS in Computer Science from Brown University.
